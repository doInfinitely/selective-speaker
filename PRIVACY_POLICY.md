# Privacy Policy for Selective Speaker

**Last Updated: November 13, 2025**

## Introduction

Selective Speaker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Selective Speaker (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you use Selective Speaker, we collect:

#### Account Information
- **Email address** - Used for authentication via Firebase
- **User ID** - Unique identifier for your account
- **Display name** - Optional profile information

#### Voice and Audio Data
- **Voice recordings** - Audio files recorded through the app
- **Transcriptions** - Text versions of your speech
- **Speaker embeddings** - Mathematical representations of your voice patterns used for speaker identification

#### Location Data
- **GPS coordinates** - Approximate location where recordings were made (optional)
- **Addresses** - Human-readable addresses derived from GPS coordinates

#### Technical Data
- **Device information** - Device model, Android version, app version
- **Timestamps** - When recordings and transcriptions were created
- **Usage analytics** - How you interact with the app

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

### Core Functionality
- **Authentication** - Verifying your identity and securing your account
- **Voice Recognition** - Training AI models to identify your voice
- **Transcription** - Converting your speech to text
- **Speaker Filtering** - Separating your voice from others in recordings
- **Data Storage** - Saving your recordings and transcripts

### Service Improvement
- **Bug fixes** - Identifying and resolving technical issues
- **Feature development** - Understanding how to improve the app
- **Performance optimization** - Enhancing app speed and reliability

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

### AssemblyAI
- **Purpose:** Speech-to-text transcription and speaker diarization
- **Data shared:** Audio recordings
- **Privacy policy:** [https://www.assemblyai.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://www.assemblyai.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- **Data retention:** Audio files are processed and deleted after transcription

### Firebase (Google)
- **Purpose:** User authentication and analytics
- **Data shared:** Email, user ID, usage statistics
- **Privacy policy:** [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

### Cloudinary
- **Purpose:** Cloud storage for audio files
- **Data shared:** Audio recordings
- **Privacy policy:** [https://cloudinary.com/privacy](https://cloudinary.com/privacy)

### OpenStreetMap Nominatim
- **Purpose:** Reverse geocoding (converting GPS to addresses)
- **Data shared:** GPS coordinates
- **Privacy policy:** [https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy](https://osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy)

### Railway/Render
- **Purpose:** Backend hosting and database storage
- **Data shared:** All app data (encrypted)
- **Privacy policy:** [https://railway.app/legal/privacy](https://railway.app/legal/privacy)

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

### Encryption
- **In transit:** All data is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **At rest:** Database and file storage are encrypted
- **Authentication:** Firebase handles secure authentication

### Access Control
- **Personal data:** Only accessible by you through your account
- **Backend access:** Secured with Firebase authentication tokens
- **Database:** Protected with access controls and authentication

### Secure Storage
- Audio files stored in secure cloud storage (Cloudinary)
- Database hosted on secure servers (Railway/Render)
- Regular security updates and patches

## Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access
- View all your recordings and transcripts in the app
- Request a copy of your data

### Deletion
- Delete individual recordings through the app
- Delete your entire account and all associated data
- Request data deletion by emailing support@selectivespeaker.app

### Portability
- Download your transcripts and recordings
- Request data export in machine-readable format

### Correction
- Update your profile information
- Correct inaccuracies in your data

### Opt-Out
- Location tracking (can be disabled in app settings)
- Analytics (can be disabled in app settings)

## Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

- **Active accounts:** Data retained until you delete it or close your account
- **Deleted recordings:** Permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Closed accounts:** All data deleted within 90 days
- **Backups:** Deleted from backups within 180 days

## Children's Privacy

Selective Speaker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## Changes to This Policy

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
